About Dr. Boitumelo Nkgudi

This was certainly the case when in his final year of high school, he expressed an interest in medicine to the family GP and was advised to consider a more attainable career goal.

"He told me that medicine was difficult and probably not for me, and I resolved there and then that I would prove him wrong," Dr. Nkgudi recalls. He went on to do just that and in a decisive fashion.

After matriculating from South African College High School, Cape Town in 2002, he earned his Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery degrees from the University of Cape Town in 2008.

Professional Qualifications

  • MBChB - University of Cape Town (2008)
  • Fellowship in General Surgery - College of Medicine of South Africa
  • Registered with the Health Professions Council of South Africa
  • Member of the South African Medical Association

Patient Information & Resources

Dr. Nkgudi specializes in a wide range of general surgical procedures. Below are some of the conditions he treats and procedures he performs.

PRURITUS ANI

Itching around the anal area that can be caused by various factors including infections, skin conditions, or hygiene issues.

PERI-ANAL ABSCESS

A collection of pus near the anus caused by infection of small anal glands.

PERI-ANAL FISSURE

A small tear in the thin, moist tissue (mucosa) that lines the anus.

PERI-ANAL FISTULA

An abnormal tunnel between the anal canal and the outer skin of the anus.

RECTAL PROLAPSE ABDOMINAL REPAIR

Surgical correction of rectal prolapse through an abdominal approach.

AMPUTATION ABOVE KNEE

Surgical removal of a leg above the knee joint.

AMPUTATION BELOW KNEE

Surgical removal of a leg below the knee joint.

AMPUTATION THROUGH KNEE

Surgical removal of a leg through the knee joint.

AMPUTATION TOE

Surgical removal of one or more toes.

HERNIA FEMORAL OPEN REPAIR

Surgical repair of a femoral hernia through an open approach.

HERNIA INCISIONAL OPEN OR LAPAROSCOPIC REPAIR

Repair of a hernia that occurs through a previous surgical incision, either open or laparoscopic.

HERNIA INGUINAL OPEN REPAIR

Traditional open surgical repair of an inguinal hernia.

TOENAIL EXCISION

Surgical removal of part or all of a toenail.

GASTRECTOMY

Partial or complete surgical removal of the stomach.

HERNIA UMBILICAL OPEN REPAIR

Open surgical repair of an umbilical hernia.

HERNIA INGUINAL LAPAROSCOPIC REPAIR

Minimally invasive laparoscopic repair of an inguinal hernia.

LUMPS AND LESIONS EXCISION

Surgical removal of abnormal lumps or lesions from the skin or subcutaneous tissue.

RECTAL PROLAPSE DELORMES REPAIR

A surgical procedure to correct rectal prolapse by mucosal sleeve resection.

APPENDISECTOMY LAPAROSCOPIC

Minimally invasive removal of the appendix.

APPENDISECTOMY OPEN

Traditional open surgical removal of the appendix.

BOWEL FISTULA REPAIR

Surgical repair of an abnormal connection between the bowel and another organ or the skin.

CLOSER STOMA

Surgical closure of a stoma (artificial opening).

COLONOSCOPY

Endoscopic examination of the large bowel and the distal part of the small bowel.

HEMORRHOIDECTOMY

Surgical removal of hemorrhoids.
